What's On Tap

Whether you're looking for a hoppy, something dark, something rich, or something new, you'll always find something to love on tap at Darkling Wood.

Our Beer

From our Canto IPA to our award-winning Dortmunder, we craft every beer with unparalleled attention to detail. We painstakingly select every batch of hops, tirelessly breed the perfect yeast strains, and never compromise quality in our quest to deliver the best possible product to our customers.

Learn more about our beers and our brewing process

Our Goal

Started as a nano-brewery in a one-car garage in Southwest Raleigh, North Carolina, we grew our business from nano to micro volume in only six months. Our goal is to be in our own brewpub and microbrewing facility by 2017. We know that to continue to grow our business, we must continue to deliver a product worthy of the support of our customers.
